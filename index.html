Regulatory timeline

44 years of hospital licensure — and counting

Pennsylvania's hospital licensure framework was last substantially modernized in 1982. Full package relief is not expected before 2029. HAP is asking DOH for three administratively feasible fixes this year — through guidance, policy statements, or coordination — so hospitals and patients do not wait the full runway.

What HAP is asking for

Written guidance, statements of policy, or interpretive letters — within DOH's existing authority — without waiting on the full rulemaking package.

The cost of waiting

Every month of delay means repeated architectural engagements, duplicative surveys, and per-facility credentialing — administrative effort diverted from patient care.
